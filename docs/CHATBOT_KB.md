# ULTRAFLEX Chatbot Knowledge Base

This document contains canonical information the chatbot should use when answering.

## Membership & Pricing
- Membership purchases and pricing are handled via Ashbourne portal: https://secure.ashbournemanagement.co.uk/signupuk/index.aspx?fn=grbh2
- Weekly and monthly passes are available; pricing varies by location.
- Dual site access is available for existing members on request: email info@ufg.

### Answer Style (for the assistant)
- Be concise. Prefer short paragraphs and bullet points.
- Never quote fixed prices on the website; always link to Ashbourne.
- When unsure about a location detail (hours/facilities), advise to check that location’s page or contact reception.

## Opening Hours
- Many sites offer 24/7 access for qualifying members; staffed hours differ.
- Typical hours (examples; confirm per location page):
  - West Leeds: Weekdays 05:30–22:00, Weekends 08:00–20:00
  - North Leeds: 06:00–22:00 (daily) with weekend variations
  - Check the specific location page for accurate hours.

## Locations
- UK: West Leeds, North Leeds, Normanton, Rotherham, York, Hull, Durham, Lincoln, West London.
- International: Athens (Greece).
- Each location offers premium equipment; amenities (boxing area, martial arts, etc.) may vary.

## Trainers & Services
- Personal training available at all gyms; see Trainers page for profiles.
- Some locations have partner services (e.g., physio, car wash). Refer to the location page.

## Contact
- General enquiries: info@ufg
- For cancellations, freezes, membership changes: use Ashbourne portal or contact gym reception.
